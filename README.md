# LucasGraphic

Photography, design & AI art portfolio by Łukasz Grochal — designer & photographer since 1996.

🌐 [lucasgraphic.com](https://lucasgraphic.com)

## Background
IT Technician by education · Adobe Certified (EU) · Studio Photography Certificate.
In 2000s worked on game development with Cinema 4D XL, then transitioned to web
development as a freelancer — HTML, CSS, PHP, Joomla, Drupal, WordPress.

## Photography
Studio, macro, drone & travel photography since 2010.
Countries: Norway, Spain, Portugal, Poland, Croatia.

## AI Art & Lab
Generative art with ComfyUI — experimental series including Arcimboldo-inspired
compositions and Podkowiński reinterpretations.
- **Models:** FLUX2.dev · SDXL · Qwen-Image

## Web Development
Dropped Ghost CMS (Node.js) in 2024 to build a custom stack from scratch.
- **Stack:** Next.js 15 · PayloadCMS · TypeScript · MongoDB · Tailwind CSS v4
- **Hosting:** Self-hosted Linux · nginx · PM2

## Tools
- **Photo/Video:** Photoshop · DaVinci Resolve · Affinity (replaced Illustrator)
- **3D/Animation:** Blender · Cinema 4D
- **AI:** ComfyUI · FLUX2.dev · SDXL · Qwen-Image
